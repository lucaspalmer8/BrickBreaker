The player starts out with three lives.
The game ends when the player loses all three lives.
When all the bricks are cleared, the level increases by one, and the player gets thier 3 lives back.
A score of n-thousand is rewarded for advancing to level n.
Hitting a brick with a circle on it will give the player an additional ball, up to a maximum of 6 balls at once.
As long as one ball is in play, the player will not lose any lives.
